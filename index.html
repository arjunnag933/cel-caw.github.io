About Campus Ambassador Program

The Campus Ambassador Program of Coalescence is a Campus-Connect Program aimed at building a network of like-minded students across India. The program assists and nurtures future leaders of the country, thus contributing to their professional development. Our goal is to empower and train a select group of students across India to serve as specialists in their respective institutions.
